About DharTee

DharTee is a dynamic professional services firm dedicated to empowering businesses and individuals with comprehensive solutions. Based in Jaipur and also serving clients in Gandhidham, Gujarat, we specialize in a unique blend of services spanning taxation, legal support, web and software development, and expert consultation on new government schemes and portals. Our mission is to provide tailored, technology-driven solutions that help our clients navigate complex challenges and achieve their strategic goals. At DharTee, we pride ourselves on our expert team, timely delivery, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction.

About Director - Dharmendra Sharma

Dharmendra Sharma, the visionary founder of DharTee, brings a rare and powerful combination of expertise to the table. As an Advocate, Tax Consultant, and GST Practitioner, he possesses a deep understanding of the legal and financial landscapes. Complementing this is his proficiency as a skilled Web and Software Developer and a Technical Expert. Originally from Barmer, Rajasthan, Dharmendra has built a reputation in Jaipur for his creative mindset and innovative use of technology. He has successfully designed numerous websites and software solutions, consulted with a diverse range of businesses to foster growth, and advised many leading coaching institutes. His passion lies in leveraging technology to simplify complexities and create opportunities for his clients.
